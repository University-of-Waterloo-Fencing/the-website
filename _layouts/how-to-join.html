How to Join

Buying a club membership

Visit the club memberships page on the Warrior Athletics Website. You must be a current student or alumni/employee with a gym membership in order to view this page.

If you are looking to attend one of our free sessions, select the "CLUB TRY-IT MEMBERSHIP". Otherwise, choose between
     ➼ "FENCING - INTRODUCTORY/BEGINNER"
     ➼ "FENCING - EQUIPMENT RENTAL"
     ➼ "FENCING - PERSONAL EQUIPMENT", or
     ➼ "FENCING (OPEN)"
based on your skill level. Then, click "ADD TO CART" and checkout to complete your purchase.
